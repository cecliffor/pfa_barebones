Craig E. Clifford, DPM, MHA
Board Certified: Foot & Ankle Surgery · Podiatric Medicine

With over a decade of surgical expertise, Dr. Clifford recognized that most foot/ankle surgery can be avoided with appropriate conservative care. In 2022, he left high-volume surgery to expand access to cutting-edge nonsurgical treatment — while still offering minimally invasive surgery when truly necessary. Every patient receives an individualized, evidence-based approach.

Dr. Clifford completed his residency at Franciscan Foot and Ankle Institute, later serving as Director of Research and Residency Training. Honors include Clinician of the Year, Instructor of the Year, and Washington State Podiatric Medical Association Physician of the Year (2020). He has served on the Board of the International Foot and Ankle Foundation and volunteered with the Yucatan Children’s Project performing deformity surgeries for underserved children in Mexico.
